Margaret Atwood

--Novelist and Social Commentator--

Born in Canada, 1939, prolific author Margaret Atwood has penned multiple award-winning novels. Her writing has also been developed into adaptations for the screen and television, the most recent notable of which being The Handmaid's Tale. Though most famous for her fiction writing, she also produces poetry and non-fiction.

Some of Atwood's most notable works include:

  • The Handmaid's Tale
  • Cat's Eye
  • Alias Grace
  • The Blind Assassin
  • Oryx and Crake
